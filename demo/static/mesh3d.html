How this demo works
Every vertex move, edge, and face boundary edit is a CRDT op synced over the same relay used by the 2D demo, just pointed at a MeshCRDT room instead of a DrawingDocument room. Toggle "Go offline", edit the mesh, then reconnect — your changes merge with anything collaborators did in the meantime with no conflicts.
